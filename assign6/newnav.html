Review

Highly recommend this place. Have ordered Butter Chicken, Murg Mussalam, Chicken Biryani and Egg Biryani from here. All items were simply exceptional and perfect. Price : 20/-

Ratings : 4

Review

One of the most delicious and the biggest Vada Pav I've ever had. Big Enough to comprise my one-time's meal. Costs Rs. 20/-. The Samosa is also a must-try. Quick service. The only con is they accept cash payments only. Very crowded during the peak hours.
